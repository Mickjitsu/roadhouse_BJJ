RoadHouse BJJ

Home to Irelands elite BJJ athletes.

Here at roadhouse BJJ we pride ourselves in being a club that supports athletes from novice to professional with the same attention, skill and care that all professionals would expect. Located in the heart of Belfast City Centre, we have members from across all sides of the community and provide a fun, judgement free place for EVERYONE to learn and better themselves.

This is roadhouse, you will be ok!

The vast majority of our team are beginners who are just starting on their JiuJitsu Journey. This is why we host a wide range of introductory and beginner orientated classes to help all members get off to a good start and really understand the fundamentals of the sport.

Enjoy the process!

WHY TRAIN AT ROADHOUSE BJJ?

No Strings Attached


We don't try to lure members with sales pitches. We offer a one week free trial with no strings attached to allow everyone to try BJJ for themselves!

Mental benefits


Brazilian Jiu Jitsu provides all those who train with many mental health benefits. From helping teach critical thinking and problem solving, to helping people learn how to keep calm, relax, breathe and act effectively under pressure.

Physical Benefits


Training Jiujitsu is a way for people to get fit and stay active in a healthy and supportive environment, while also helping people learn vital self-defence skills.
