ABOUT US

Our Team

Founded in 2024 by head coach Eddie Bravo, our team here at Roadhouse has grown significantly. With 100+ members and over 10 dedicated coaches, we are taking the Irish BJJ scene by storm.

Our Classes

We provide classes in Brazilian JiuJitsu (All Levels), Wrestling, Muay Thai, Judo, and Strength & Conditioning! These classes are suitable for beginners, hobbyists, and professional competitors alike!


Our Facilities

We not only have a large indoor training area for martial arts, but our gym also includes an outdoor strength and conditioning area, indoor sauna, and ice baths free to all our members!
